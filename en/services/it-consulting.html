IT strategy and architecture

Our IT consulting services help organizations design and implement technology strategies aligned with business objectives. From assessing your current infrastructure to designing modern cloud architectures, we work with your team to identify improvement opportunities, cost optimization and adoption of new technologies.

Consulting areas

Cloud Architecture Design

Scalable, resilient and secure Azure and AWS architecture design. Landing zones, governance, networking and multi-cloud strategies.

Cost Optimization

Cloud cost analysis, rightsizing, identification of unused resources, reservation strategies and savings plans. FinOps and chargeback.

IT Processes & Governance

ITIL process implementation: incident, change, problem, configuration management. Service catalog, CMDB and continuous improvement.

Digital Transformation

Cloud adoption strategy, application modernization, DevOps, CI/CD, infrastructure as code and IT process automation.
